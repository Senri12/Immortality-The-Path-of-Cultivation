# 📜 CurseForge Description & Changelog

### 🌟 Version 1.2.0 — The Cultivation Structures & Spirit Beast Expansion

#### 🏛️ 3 New Ancient Cultivation Structures
- **Ancient Pagoda of Trials (`immortality:ancient_pagoda`)**:
  - A 3-story eastern pagoda made of Deepslate Bricks and Jade.
  - **Ground Floor**: Meditation Mat, Enlightenment Altar, and beginner cultivation supplies.
  - **Second Floor**: Library containing secret manuals (`Wandering Cloud`, `Crimson Flame`, `Stone Body`).
  - **Third Floor & Roof**: Jade Infusion Altar, Jade Pedestals, Formation Core, and a guardian Spirit Beast.
- **Ruined Dao Shrine (`immortality:ruined_dao_shrine`)**:
  - An open-air circular sanctuary platform with Jade pillars, Bamboo/Jade Flags, and an active `Formation Core`.
  - Holds chests with array runes (`Taiji Shield`, `Spirit Convergence`) and rare materials (`Dragon Vein Stone`, `Heavenly Iron`).
- **Spirit Vein Grotto (`immortality:spirit_vein_grotto`)**:
  - An underground cavern featuring a spiritual water pool, Jade blocks, Spirit Stone veins, and wild vegetation.
  - Houses a central island with an Enlightenment Altar, rare cultivation pills, and a resident Spirit Beast.

#### 🐾 Custom Entity: Spirit Beast (`immortality:spirit_beast`)
- **Attributes**: 50 HP (25 Hearts), 8 Damage, 0.28 Speed, 4 Armor.
- **Abilities**: Attacks inflict **Qi Sealing** (Slowness & Weakness) with Soul Flame & Qi particle bursts.
- **Loot**: Drops 1–2 Spirit Beast Cores guaranteed, plus elemental cores, Spirit Grass, and Spirit Stones.
- **Natural Spawning**: Rare spawns in Overworld biomes and `World of Immortals` alongside balanced vanilla undead.

#### ⚔️ 16 New Items & Cultivation Reagents
- **Cores & Reagents**: Lightning Beast Core, Frost Beast Core, Spirit Grass, Dragon Vein Stone, Heavenly Iron, Phoenix Feather, Lightning Talisman.
- **Pills**: Foundation Pill, Golden Core Pill.
- **Manual**: Nine Dragons Manual.
- **6 Array Runes**: Heavenly Lightning Rune, Frost Domain Rune, Life Spring Rune, Gravity Suppression Rune, Flame Lotus Rune, Qi Sealing Rune.
