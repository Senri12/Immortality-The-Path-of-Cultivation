# ☯️ Cultivation Stages, Breakthroughs & Research Guide

Cultivation is the central character-progression process in the mod.

---

## ☯️ 1. Cultivation Stages

The mod has **12 Cultivation Stages** in total:

1. `MORTAL` — **Mortal** (Max Qi: 0)
2. `QI_GATHERING` — **Qi Gathering** (Max Qi: 100)
3. `FOUNDATION_ESTABLISHMENT` — **Foundation Establishment** (Max Qi: 250)
4. `CORE_FORMATION` — **Core Formation** (Max Qi: 500)
5. `NASCENT_SOUL` — **Nascent Soul** (Max Qi: 1000)
6. `SPIRIT_SEVERING` — **Spirit Severing** (Max Qi: 2000)
7. `ASCENDANT` — **Ascendant** (Max Qi: 4000)
8. `ILLUSORY_YIN` — **Illusory Yin** (Max Qi: 7500)
9. `CORPOREAL_YANG` — **Corporeal Yang** (Max Qi: 12000)
10. `NIRVANA_SCRYER` — **Nirvana Scryer** (Max Qi: 20000)
11. `NIRVANA_CLEANSER` — **Nirvana Cleanser** (Max Qi: 35000)
12. `VOID_TRIBULANT` — **Void Tribulant** (Max Qi: 50000)

---

## ⚡ 2. Breakthrough

- Command: `/cultivation breakthrough`
- A breakthrough requires accumulating 100% of your max Qi, as well as completing the necessary research or using ancient pills (`Foundation Pill`, `Golden Core Pill`).
- A successful breakthrough raises your Qi reserve, passive attributes, and Spiritual Sense radius.

## 🔬 4. Research Board & Study

- Craft the research board (`Research Study Board`).
- Prepare researches and complete them by spending Insight points and spiritual ingredients.
- Study commands: `/cultivation research next` or `/cultivation research <id>`.
