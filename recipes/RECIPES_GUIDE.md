## 🔮 Cultivation Requirements for Infusions

Some infusions on the Jade Infusion Altar (`Jade Infusion Altar`) require a certain cultivator stage. If your cultivation status isn't high enough, the ritual on the altar **will not start**, and you'll get a warning.

| Cultivation Stage Hierarchy | Priority |
|-----------------------------|-----------|
| 1. **Mortal** | Tier 0 |
| 2. **Qi Gathering** | Tier 1 |
| 3. **Foundation Establishment** | Tier 2 |
| 4. **Core Formation** | Tier 3 |
| 5. **Nascent Soul** | Tier 4 |
| 6. **Spirit Severing** | Tier 5 |

## 🌀 Array Formations

To build an array, link 3 or more flags (`Bamboo Flag` / `Jade Flag`) to a **Formation Core** (`Formation Core`) using the **Formation Compass** (`Formation Compass`), then insert the corresponding rune.

| # | Rune Name | Array Effect | Particles |
|---|---------------|----------------|---------|
| 1 | **Spirit Convergence Rune** *(Qi Gathering)* | Restores +3 Qi to players in range while meditating | `HAPPY_VILLAGER` |
| 2 | **Taiji Shield Rune** *(Taiji Dome)* | Reflects projectiles back and pushes hostile mobs away from the center | `END_ROD` |
| 3 | **Mirage Concealment Rune** *(Phantom Mist)* | Grants invisibility to players and applies Blindness to monsters | `WITCH` |
| 4 | **Sword Forest Rune** *(Thousand Swords)* | Deals magic sword damage (8.0 HP) to all enemies in range | `CRIT` |
| 5 | **Heavenly Lightning Rune** *(Heavenly Lightning)* | Strikes enemies with lightning (10.0 HP) with a thunder sound | `ELECTRIC_SPARK` |
| 6 | **Frost Domain Rune** *(Frost Domain)* | Freezes enemies, deals cold damage (5.0 HP), and applies Slowness IV | `SNOWFLAKE` |
| 7 | **Life Spring Rune** *(Life Spring)* | Applies Regeneration II and Saturation to cultivators in range | `HEART` |
| 8 | **Gravity Suppression Rune** *(Gravity Suppression)* | Pulls flying/jumping enemies to the ground and applies Slowness III | `ANGRY_VILLAGER` |
| 9 | **Flame Lotus Rune** *(Flame Lotus)* | Ignites enemies for 4 sec and deals fire damage (7.0 HP) | `FLAME` |
| 10 | **Qi Sealing Rune** *(Qi Sealing)* | Weakens enemies (Weakness II + Slowness) and reduces players' Qi Deviation | `SOUL_FIRE_FLAME` |
